About

Elgin-Skye McLaren is a Masters of Science student in the Tangible Computing Lab at Simon Fraser University. Designer, developer, researcher, Elgin-Skye's interest are in Brain Computer Interfacing, Embodied Computing, Sound and Artificial Intelligence.

Elgin-Skye McLaren is a creative coder and researcher based in Vancouver, BC. She is currently pursuing her M.Sc. at the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University.

Elgin's research interests include: Human-centered design, brain-computer interfacing, design evaluation, and technological embodiment within artistic performance.
