About

Elgin-Skye McLaren is a creative coder and researcher based in Vancouver, BC. She is currently an Msc student in Simon Fraser University's School of Interactive Arts and Technology. Her research interests are Brain Computer Interfacing, Embodied Computing, Sound, and Artificial Intelligence.

Elgin's research interests include: Human-centered design, brain-computer interfacing, design evaluation, and technological embodiment within artistic performance.
